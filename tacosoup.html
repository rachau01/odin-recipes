Taco Soup

Taco Soup is a body warming soup that is sure to please. Basically and dump and go meal that can be done in a crock pot or stock pot.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb ground beef
  • 2 cans Ranch beans
  • 3 cans pinto beans drained
  • 2 cans crushed tomatoes undrained
  • 2 cans Rotel
  • 3 cans whole kernel corn
  • beef broth
  • ketchup
  • 2 packets taco seasoning

Steps

  1. Brown ground beef, and drain.
  2. Combine all ingredients in a stock pot.
  3. Cook on simmer for 30-45 minutes, longer if desired
  4. For crockpot, cook on low for 6hours for best results.
  5. Add cheese, sour cream, and chips if desired.
