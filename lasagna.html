Lasagna

Shortcut Lasagna is sure to be a family favorite. It is easy to make and feeds a crowd. Cheesy, saucy, meety goodness.

Ingredients

  • uncooked lasagna noodles
  • spagetti sauce
  • 1 lb ground beef
  • 1lb Italian sausage
  • cheddar cheese, shredded
  • mozarella cheese, shredded
  • parmesan cheese, shredded
  • garlic salt
  • italian seasoning

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
  2. Brown ground beef and italian sausage, and drain.
  3. Boil lasagna noodles until eldente.
  4. Add cooked beef, saugage, and spagetti sauce into a skillet and cook on low heat.
  5. Add garlic salt and italian seasoning too taste.
  6. Layer noodles, sauce and meat mixture, and cheese in a 13x9 baking dish.
  7. Cook for 35-45 minutes.
