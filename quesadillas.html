Quesadillas

This quesadilla recipe is simple and easy. Just meat, cheese, and tortilas are needed to make your family happy.

Ingredients

  • 2 lb ground beef
  • 2 packets taco seasoning
  • burrito size tortilas
  • shreded cheddar cheese

Steps

  1. Brown ground beef, and drain.
  2. Combine cooked ground beef with taco seasoning and cook according to directions on packet.
  3. In a hot skillet, add a tad of olive oil.
  4. Lay tortilla down in skillet and add meat and cheese to half, folding tortilla over.
  5. Cook on both sides til lightly browned.
  6. Can serve with sour cream and taco sauce, if desired.
